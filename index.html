Sepehr Sepanta Giti:

Sepehr Sepanta Giti (SSG) is an international company actively dealing with Aero industry including General Aviation, Aviation Engineering, Airtaxi and International Aviation Commerce.

SSG aviation group offers wide spectrum of aviation related service and products. It makes revenue from light passenger product (including two-seat, four-seat and six-seat aircraft for training, cruise or recreational use), fixed wing drones (from 5 kg to 150 kg with a 7-hour flight range) and hybrid vertical drones (from 1 kg to 50 kg), hot air balloons as well as aircraft maintenance services. These drones are mainly utilized for pesticide and fertilizer spraying in agricultural fields, photogrammetry, infrastructure inspection, oil and pipeline monitoring.

Cooperation on various projects in the international scale along with advanced engineering facilities and highly talented academic staffs have broaden the company’s relations and provided us with a great deal of expertise in this way. The company is intended to bridge the gap between university outcome and industry demands through close cooperation with academics and experts.

SSG does its best to contribute to development of aviation industry as well as enhancement of aviation knowledge.

In Sepehr Sepanta Giti aviation group, we appreciate innovation and lay the foundation for future developments in aero industry.
